    What is Go!™ ?

    It is a search engine. It was created in December 30, 2022.

    Is it private and safe?

    Of course! we have no idea of your history, searches etc. The search engine does not offer malicious, inappropriate and other sites. We want it to be safe, appropriate and suitable for all types of people and all ages.

    Is the search engine based on an API and/or on another search engine?

    Yes! We use an API from Google! That is to say that the results are provided by Google, but modified by us! We are not the only search engine that uses a google API! We have StartPage, Clickette and more than you might think.
