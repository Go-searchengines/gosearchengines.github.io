Add Search Box to your website

Copy and paste the code below into your website's pages to display a Go! search box. 

   
   
     <form method="get" action="https://gosearch.ga/search.html">
	<div>
		<a href="https://gosearch.ga"> 
			<img src="https://gosearch.ga/Untitled.png" style="height:100px;padding-bottom:1px;">
		</a>
	</div>
	<input size="30" name="q" placeholder="Search the web with Go!...">  
	<input type="submit" value="Search"> 
</form>

For more questions or comments about this feature, contact Go! Group at gosearch@gosearch.ga, mention @gosearchoff on your tweet or DM on Twitter.
